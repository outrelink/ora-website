Subscriber Agreement

Last updated: December 10, 2025

Important: By subscribing to ORA, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined in this agreement. Please read this document carefully before subscribing.

1. Subscription Plans

ORA offers the following subscription plans:

  • • Free: Basic features with limited functionality
  • • Essentials: Monthly subscription with essential features for content creators
  • • Pro: Monthly subscription with advanced features and analytics
  • • Elite: Monthly subscription with premium features and priority support

All paid subscriptions are billed monthly through Apple's App Store. Subscription prices are subject to change with 30 days notice.

2. Payment Terms

Payment and billing are handled entirely through Apple's App Store:

  • • Payment Method: All payments are processed through your Apple ID account
  • • Billing Cycle: Subscriptions are billed monthly on the same date each month
  • • Auto-Renewal: Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • • Price Changes: We will notify you of any price changes at least 30 days in advance. If you do not agree to the new price, you may cancel your subscription
  • • Taxes: Prices displayed do not include applicable taxes. Taxes are calculated and charged by Apple based on your location

3. Subscription Activation

Your subscription becomes active immediately upon successful payment:

  • • Immediate Access: Once payment is confirmed by Apple, you gain immediate access to your subscribed plan features
  • • Verification: We verify your subscription with Apple's servers to ensure payment was successful
  • • Sync Across Devices: Your subscription status syncs across all devices when you sign in with the same account
  • • Activation Time: In most cases, activation occurs within seconds. In rare cases, it may take up to 24 hours

4. Cancellation and Refunds

You may cancel your subscription at any time:

  • • How to Cancel: Cancel through your Apple ID account settings or the App Store. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → ORA → Cancel Subscription
  • • Effective Date: Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You retain access until the period ends
  • • No Refunds: We do not provide refunds for partial subscription periods. Apple's refund policy applies to all purchases
  • • Apple Refund Policy: For refund requests, contact Apple Support. Refunds are subject to Apple's terms and conditions
  • • Immediate Cancellation: If you cancel during a billing period, you will continue to have access until the end of that period

5. Subscription Renewal

Your subscription will automatically renew unless cancelled:

  • • Auto-Renewal: Subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing period
  • • Renewal Date: You will be charged on the same date each month
  • • Payment Failure: If payment fails, your subscription will be suspended until payment is successful
  • • Notification: Apple will notify you before each renewal charge
  • • Price Changes: If the subscription price increases, you will be notified and can choose to cancel

6. Restore Purchases

You can restore your previous purchases:

  • • Restore Button: Use the "Restore Purchases" button in the app to restore subscriptions purchased on other devices
  • • Same Apple ID: Purchases are tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device using the same Apple ID
  • • Verification: Restored purchases are verified with Apple's servers to confirm validity
  • • Active Subscriptions: Only active subscriptions can be restored. Expired subscriptions cannot be restored

7. Service Availability

We strive to provide continuous service, but cannot guarantee uninterrupted access:

  • • Uptime: We aim for 99.9% uptime but do not guarantee uninterrupted service
  • • Maintenance: Scheduled maintenance may temporarily interrupt service. We will provide notice when possible
  • • No Refunds for Downtime: Service interruptions do not entitle you to refunds or credits
  • • Feature Changes: We may modify, add, or remove features at any time. Core features will remain available

8. Account Requirements

To subscribe, you must meet the following requirements:

  • • Apple ID: You must have a valid Apple ID with payment method on file
  • • Age Requirement: You must be at least 13 years old (or age of majority in your jurisdiction)
  • • Account: You must create an ORA account to sync your subscription across devices
  • • Device: Subscriptions are available on iOS devices only

9. Prohibited Uses

You agree not to:

  • • Share your subscription with others or attempt to circumvent payment
  • • Use the service for any illegal or unauthorized purpose
  • • Attempt to reverse engineer or hack the service
  • • Transfer your subscription to another Apple ID
  • • Use automated tools to access the service

10. Termination

We may terminate your subscription if:

  • • You violate this agreement or our Terms of Service
  • • Payment fails repeatedly
  • • You engage in fraudulent activity
  • • We discontinue the service (with 30 days notice)

Upon termination, you will lose access to premium features immediately, but your data will be retained according to our Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Agreement

We may update this subscriber agreement from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or through the app at least 30 days before they take effect. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated agreement.
