Email Templates

Brand Pitch Template

Subject: Collaboration Opportunity with [Your Name] Hi [Brand Name] Team, I hope this email finds you well! My name is [Your Name], and I'm a [niche] content creator with [X] followers on [platform(s)]. I've been following [Brand Name] for a while and love [specific thing about brand]. I believe my audience would be genuinely interested in your products/services, and I'd love to explore a potential collaboration. Here's what I can offer: - [Content type: Instagram post, TikTok video, YouTube video, etc.] - [Deliverables: e.g., 1 feed post + 3 stories] - [Timeline: e.g., Ready to post within 2 weeks] My audience demographics: - [Age range, location, interests] - Average engagement rate: [X]% - [Any other relevant stats] I'm open to discussing rates and deliverables that work for both of us. Would you be available for a quick call this week? Looking forward to hearing from you! Best regards, [Your Name] [Your Email] [Your Social Media Handles]

Follow-up Template

Subject: Following Up: [Original Subject] Hi [Name], I wanted to follow up on my previous email about [collaboration opportunity]. I know you're probably busy, but I wanted to make sure this didn't get lost in your inbox. I'm still very interested in working with [Brand Name] and would love to discuss how we can create something amazing together. Would you be available for a quick 15-minute call this week? I'm flexible with timing. If now isn't the right time, I completely understand. Just let me know when would be better to reconnect. Best regards, [Your Name]

Rate Negotiation Template

Subject: Re: Collaboration Proposal - Rate Discussion Hi [Name], Thank you for the collaboration opportunity! I'm excited about working with [Brand Name]. Regarding the rate, I've calculated my standard rate for this type of collaboration based on: - Content creation time: [X] hours - My reach and engagement: [X] followers, [X]% engagement rate - Industry benchmarks for creators in my niche - Usage rights requested My rate for this collaboration would be $[X] for [deliverables]. I'm open to discussing this further and finding a rate that works for both of us. I'm also flexible on deliverables if that helps. Would you like to schedule a call to discuss? Best regards, [Your Name]
