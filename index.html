What is ORA?

ORA is a comprehensive business management platform built exclusively for content creators. We understand the unique challenges creators face, from negotiating fair rates to managing multiple brand partnerships, tracking revenue across platforms, and protecting your rights in contracts.

Whether you're an Instagram influencer, YouTube creator, TikTok star, or multi platform content creator, ORA provides the tools you need to run your creator business professionally and efficiently.

Our mission is to empower creators with the same business tools and insights that traditional businesses have, so you can focus on what you do best, creating amazing content.

Everything You Need to Run Your Creator Business

Powerful tools designed to help you calculate rates, manage deals, protect your rights, and grow your revenue

Smart Rate Calculator

Get instant, data driven rate suggestions based on your reach, engagement, content type, and industry benchmarks. Never undervalue your work again.

Deal Management

Track all your brand partnerships in one place. Manage negotiations, deadlines, deliverables, and payments with ease.

AI Contract Analysis

Upload contracts and get instant analysis of red flags, unfair terms, and negotiation points. Protect your rights with AI powered insights.

Brand Vault

Research and save information about brands you've worked with or want to work with. Track ratings, payment history, and creator reviews.

Opportunity Pipeline

Track potential brand collaborations from first contact to closed deal. Manage your sales pipeline with stages, follow ups, and communications all in one place.

Revenue Tracking

Track all your income streams in one dashboard. Get insights into your earnings trends and plan for tax season.
