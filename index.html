About ORA

Empowering Creators Worldwide

ORA is a professional platform designed exclusively for influencers and content creators. We provide enterprise-grade tools to calculate fair rates using real-time market data, manage brand collaborations efficiently, track revenue across all platforms, and protect your business with AI-powered contract analysis.

Our rate calculator uses the metrics you log such as followers, engagement, platform, deliverables, niche, and scope, then combines them with ORA’s benchmark database to recommend a fair range. Adjust assumptions like usage rights or timeline and you will instantly see how the suggested rate changes.

ORA lets you track campaigns across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Snapchat, and more by logging your stats manually or importing spreadsheets. Automated integrations are on our roadmap, and we’ll roll them out as soon as platform APIs allow.

Security is our top priority. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256), secure cloud storage with regular backups, and full GDPR compliance. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, never shared with third parties without explicit consent, and you have full control with data export and deletion options. We're SOC 2 compliant and undergo regular security audits.
