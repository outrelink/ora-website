Community Guidelines

ORA House Rules

ORA was built for professional creators. These guidelines outline how to use ORA's tools responsibly, protect brand relationships, and keep our community secure. They apply to the mobile app, web dashboard, admin tools, and any other ORA service.

1. Accurate, Verifiable Data

Record only real follower counts, earnings, payment statuses, deliverables, and contract details. Label deals accurately (pending, paid, overdue) and avoid inflating analytics. False data leads to incorrect insights and can trigger account review.

2. Confidentiality & Sensitive Information

Contracts, pricing, talent terms, and brand contacts are confidential. You may export your own data for personal records, but do not share ORA templates, clauses, or proprietary rates unless the brand has granted written permission.

3. Professional Conduct Across Channels

Support tickets, bug reports, marketing replies, and waitlist responses go directly to the ORA team. Keep all communication respectful, concise, and relevant. Abuse, spam, or harassment results in immediate review.

4. Account Integrity

  • ORA accounts and subscriptions are personal licenses.
  • Do not sell, rent, or share credentials or premium dashboards with other creators, agencies, or clients.
  • If you manage multiple team members, contact ORA for approved multi-seat access.

5. Security Expectations

Use strong passwords, enable Face ID/PIN on devices, and log out from shared hardware. Report suspicious activity immediately to official.myora.co@outlook.com. ORA may temporarily lock accounts to protect sensitive data.

6. Legal & Platform Compliance

You are responsible for following FTC disclosure rules, platform policies (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, etc.), and regional advertising laws. ORA will not assist with concealing sponsored content, fabricating proof of delivery, or other fraudulent behavior.

7. Fraud Prevention

Submitting forged contracts, fake screenshots, invented brand deals, or using ORA dashboards to sell results you did not achieve is prohibited. Violations may lead to immediate suspension or termination.

8. AI & Tool Usage

  • ORA's AI outputs, contract analysis, and calculators are licensed for your own campaigns.
  • Do not resell ORA's AI as your own "agency service" or rebrand our outputs.
  • Automated scraping, mass exporting, or training external AI models on ORA data is not allowed.

9. Reporting Misuse

If you discover abuse, data leaks, or security issues, use the in-app bug report or contact official.myora.co@outlook.com with details (screenshots, timeline, severity).

10. Enforcement

ORA may warn, suspend, restrict, or terminate accounts that violate these rules or the Terms of Service. Severe cases (fraud, data theft, harassment, policy evasion) may result in immediate termination and cooperation with legal authorities.

Questions?

For clarification or to request multi-seat access, reach out to official.myora.co@outlook.com.
